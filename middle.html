Passwords

A form of input to confirm a user's identity

Passwords started as a string of text no longer than 8 characters.

So, most passwords had words like love and god and of course: password

Password Requirements

With so little combinations, it was easy for password hackers to guess passwords.

So gradually more and more requirements were necessary to lock in a password.

ie: at least one number or one special character or one capital letter.

Now, generally, passwords are a string of numbers, letters, and special characters

abc1234!

iloveyou1

88888888

999999999
